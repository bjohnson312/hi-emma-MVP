## Getting Started

### First-Time Setup

1. **Navigate to Care Team**
   - Click "Care Team" in the sidebar
   - You'll be greeted with the setup flow

2. **Step Through Each Category**
   - Read the description and examples
   - Click "Add [Category] Member" to add someone
   - Fill in their information (only name is required)
   - Click "Add to Team" when done

3. **Skip or Continue**
   - Optional categories can be skipped
   - Required categories need at least one member
   - Your progress is saved automatically

4. **Complete Setup**
   - Review your team in the final step
   - Click "Complete Setup" to finish
   - Unlock your Care Team milestone! üéâ

### Adding Members Later

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I have to add everyone at once?**
A: No! The setup flow saves progress. Add people as you have their information.

**Q: What if I don't have someone's email?**
A: That's okay! Add them without an email and we'll remind you to add it later.

**Q: Can I add multiple primary care doctors?**
A: Yes, just add them one at a time in the Primary Care step.

**Q: How do I mark someone as primary?**
A: Use the "primary" checkbox when adding or editing a member.

**Q: Can I delete someone permanently?**
A: Members are soft-deleted (deactivated) rather than permanently removed for record-keeping.

**Q: What happens to my milestone if I remove members?**
A: Your milestone stays achieved. Milestones are never removed once earned.

**Q: Can I export my care team?**
A: Yes! Use the Export & Share feature to create a PDF of your care team.
